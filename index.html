I am currently pursuing a PhD in Astronomy and Astrophysics at the University of Chicago. I study the circumgalactic gas around galaxies outside the Milky Way. Leveraging quasar spectra from the Hubble Space Telescope, I characterize the kinematics, chemistry, and thermodynamics of intervening gas clumps. I follow these studies up with observations from the Magellan and and Very Large Telescopes to associate the identified absorbers to galaxies surrounding the quasar line-of-sight.
